The Evolution of CyberNutz

This is the evolution of CyberNutz on Twitter.

March 2021

It all started here, March 2021. CyberNutz created his first ever Twitter account named @NutzCyber. It was based around his interests at the time, Friday Night Funkin' and SM64JS.

August 2021

Joining 5 months later, @NutzCyber2 was born. This was a random alt account for CyberNutz.

December 2021

@CyberNutz123 was created, back to the Michealsoft Binbows profile picture.

After December 2021

@CyberNutz69 was created after this time! You can't access it though...
